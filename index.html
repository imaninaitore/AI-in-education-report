Introduction to artificial intelligence

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is changing education by making learning easier and more interactive. AI tools can help students learn faster, assist teachers with tasks and provide personalized support. Today, many schools and learning platforms use AI to improve the learning experience and make education more effective.

Benefits and challenges

Benefits of artificial intelligence

  1. Personalized learning for students
  2. Faster access to information
  3. Improves accessibility for students with disabilities
  4. Helps students learn at their own pace
  5. Makes learning more interactive
  6. Saves teachers time through automated grading
Challenges of Artificial intelligence
  • Privacy and data security concerns
  • Overdependence on technology
  • Internet access limitations
  • Reduced face-to-face interaction
  • Possibility of cheating or misuse
